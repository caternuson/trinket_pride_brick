KANJI CLOCK

ESP8266 based NTP clock with kanji digits.

Displays the current time using kanji number icons on 8x8 LED matrices. Based on a ESP8266 HUZZAH and LED matrices from Adafruit. Housed in a custom built wood and glass enclosure.

HARDWARE

Here are some details about the clock hardware. The overall parts are shown below.

The LEDs are tied to wooden sticks held together by round wooden end pieces as shown below.

The LED holder is inserted into the glass vase. Power and I2C cables are routed through the wooden end blocks.

All parts are brought together and secured using fasteners.
